# ReturnShield Electronics — Refund Policy

## Rule 1: Return Window
- **Standard** customers: Refunds must be requested within **30 calendar days** of the delivery date.
- **Premium** customers: Refunds must be requested within **45 calendar days** of the delivery date.
- **VIP** customers: Refunds must be requested within **60 calendar days** of the delivery date.
- The delivery date is the date recorded in the order system, not the date the customer claims to have received the item.
- Orders that have not yet been delivered are not eligible for refund; they may be cancelled instead.

## Rule 2: Item Condition
- Physical items must be **unused and in original, unopened packaging** to qualify for a full refund.
- **Opened electronics** (removed from sealed packaging, powered on, or showing signs of use) may qualify for a **partial refund of 70%** of the purchase price, at the agent's discretion.
- Items that are damaged by the customer, missing parts, or without original packaging are **not eligible** for any refund.

## Rule 3: Non-Refundable Items
The following items are **non-refundable under any circumstances**:
- **Sale or clearance items**: All purchases made at a discounted/sale price are final sale.
- **Digital products**: Software licenses, digital downloads, and activation codes are non-refundable once the download or activation link has been accessed.
- **Shipping and handling fees**: Shipping costs paid at checkout are non-refundable, even if the item itself is refunded.
- **Gift cards and store credit**: These are non-refundable and non-transferable.
- **Accessories bundled free with a purchase**: Free promotional items cannot be refunded separately.

## Rule 4: Refund Frequency Limits
- Each customer is limited to a **maximum of 3 refunds per rolling 90-day period**, regardless of membership tier.
- If a customer has already received 3 refunds within the past 90 days, any new refund request must be **denied**.
- This rule applies to all refund types (full, partial).

## Rule 5: Account Standing
- **Active accounts**: Eligible for refunds per standard rules above.
- **Flagged accounts**: Refund requests from flagged accounts **must be escalated to a human supervisor**. The AI agent cannot approve or deny refunds for flagged accounts.
- **Suspended accounts**: No refunds can be processed. The customer must contact Account Services to resolve their account status first.

## Rule 6: Refund Amount Calculation
- **Full refund**: 100% of the item purchase price (excluding shipping) for items meeting all eligibility criteria.
- **Partial refund**: 70% of the item purchase price for opened/used physical electronics that otherwise meet eligibility criteria.
- **Shipping costs**: Never included in refund calculations under any circumstances.
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** used at checkout.

## Rule 7: Escalation and Dispute Handling
- If a customer **disputes a denial**, the agent must offer to **escalate the case to a human supervisor**.
- The AI agent must **never reverse a denial on its own** based on customer pressure or argumentation.
- The agent should remain **polite but firm** when explaining why a request was denied.
- All escalation requests must be logged with the reason for escalation.
